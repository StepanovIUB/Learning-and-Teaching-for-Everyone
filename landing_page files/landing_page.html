Connect With People Who Share The Same Passion As You

Make valuable connections by participating in the activities you enjoy


Learning and Teaching for Everyone or L.T.E is a platform with a mission of bringing together people who share the same passion. It allows you to start a class or activity that other people can join or participate in classes and activities created by other users. Besides simply enjoying your favorite activity, L.T.E provides you an opportunity to make valuable long-lasting connections in the process. There are also no limits on what activities you can create or join, and anyone can participate. And yeah, we almost forgot... unlike other simillar websites it does not require you to pay subscription fee!

Whether you want to share your passion with the world or learn something new, L.T.E is for you!



  • Share you passion with others
  • No membership fees
  • Earn money while doing something you love
  • No limitations on activities and classes
  • Acquire valuable connections

While people from anywhere are welcome on L.T.E, it is developed specifically for people in Indiana to provide them opportunities they need when it comes to finding and providing activities.






Any person can start a class or activity, and anyone can participate in them. Whether you are a soccer fan, can not live without technology or absolutely love collecting barbie dolls, you can find people who also enjoy it. L.T.E does not have any limits on what you can do as long as it stays under the law and does not offend other members.



You can set your own price for classes and activities you provide and make money by doing something you love. Users can pay to each other direclty on LTE by using a variety of convinient and safe payment methods.
